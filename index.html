Meet Naksh Gupta

Cinematic Wedding Film Editor

I'm Naksh — a dedicated wedding film editor based in Lucknow, India, working under the banner of ZoomVista. Over the past 3+ years, I've crafted over 300 wedding trailers, each one a unique love story told through cinematic editing, precise color grading, and soulful music synchronization.

Although my formal name is Priyanshu Gupta, I am widely known in the industry by my nickname, Naksh Gupta. My journey into cinematic editing began with SD Photography, where I learned the fundamentals and honed my craft. I remain deeply thankful for the opportunity to work alongside them and elevate my skills to a professional level.

My craft goes beyond cuts and transitions — I create emotional experiences that couples relive for a lifetime. From grand Indian weddings to intimate celebrations, I bring a cinematic vision that transforms raw footage into timeless films.
